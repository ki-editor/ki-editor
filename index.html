Ki Editor

Introduction

Ki 1 Editor is a modal 2 editor that supports multi-cursor structural editing.

1

Pronounced as /kɪ/, not /kaɪ/. It means "tree" (木) in Japanese.

2

Modal: root word mode, meaning multiple modes.
